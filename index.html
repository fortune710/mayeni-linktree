About Mayeni Limited

  • Mayeni Limited is a diversified company that operates in the automotive, real estate, and tech sectors. It specializes in automobile transactions, acquiring and developing land into rental properties, and has plans to extend its operations into the tech industry.
  • Mayeni Limited upholds the core values of Integrity, Customer Satisfaction, Innovation, and Sustainability. It believes in conducting business with utmost honesty and transparency, ensuring customer satisfaction, staying ahead of the industry trends through innovation, and promoting sustainable practices in all its operations.
  • Mayeni Limited serves a broad range of customers. In the automotive sector, its customers include individual car buyers, fleet managers, and businesses in need of vehicles. The real estate branch of the business serves individuals and families seeking rental properties, as well as investors looking for real estate opportunities. As the company expands into the tech industry, it expects to serve tech companies, startups, and individual consumers who are interested in innovative technology solutions.
